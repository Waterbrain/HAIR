## Mascot — Hairy Hank

**Canonical reference:** [`hank-master-v8-final.png`](./hank-master-v8-final.png) ✅ **LOCKED 2026-05-08**

This is THE canonical Hank. Every future illustration of the mascot — scenario renders, marketing assets, in-product illustrations — must match this character design. When in doubt, hold the new render side-by-side with this one. If a detail differs from this reference, the new render is wrong.

The v8 render is the final locked mascot. Every future Hank illustration should match its character design:

- ✅ Massive Sailor Jerry flame hair (red→orange→yellow), 2x+ body height, with visible fur strand texture
- ✅ Receded mature hairline, pronounced bald forehead
- ✅ Plastic-troll face: bulbous nose, big round shiny eyes with catchlights, prominent ears, plastic-doll skin sheen, mischievous knowing smirk, subtle laugh lines
- ✅ Thin gold wireframe glasses sitting low on the bulbous nose
- ✅ Tobacco-brown leather barber smock with brass rivets, two patch pockets, and a small red "HAIR" patch on the left chest
- ✅ Underneath: white polo collar (visible at neck), tan cargo shorts (visible at hem with side pockets), white New Balance dad sneakers, white crew socks pulled up
- ✅ Belly button visible where the smock pulls open at the midriff (subtle troll homage)
- ✅ Sailor Jerry tattoo flash style: bold black outlines, flat color fills, white highlight pops
